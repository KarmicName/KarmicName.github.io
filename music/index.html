These are FamiTracker compositions.

I felt like I needed to get that out into the open.

As it stands, I'm relatively inexperienced in the composition of musical themes, but I do know my way around a tracker. All of these pieces were my first attempts at tracker based compositional pieces with various bits of inspiration dragged from here and there. I would definitely like to improve my compositional skills in the future.

Muspeilheim My first compositional piece. No real melody, just a basic beat and triangle wave. Not awful though. Icyheim My second compositional piece, with an attempt at melody and harmony. Doesn't loop well. Sewerheim A more experimental composition I tried, and my third. It's special. Officeheim The last piece I was working on. Show improvement, but it was unfinished.
